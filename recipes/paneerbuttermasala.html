Paneer Butter Masala



Description

Paneer Butter Masala Recipe is one of India’s most popular paneer preparation. This restaurant style recipe with soft paneer cubes dunked in a creamy, lightly spiced tomato sauce or gravy is a best one that I have been making for a long time. This rich dish is best served with roti or chapati, paratha, naan or rumali roti


Ingredients


For cashew paste

  1. ▢ 18 to 20 cashews – whole
  2. ▢ ⅓ cup hot water – for soaking cashews

For tomato puree

  1. ▢ 1 inch ginger – peeled and roughly chopped
  2. ▢ 3 to 4 garlic cloves – small to medium-sized, peeled

Other ingredients

  1. ▢ 2 tablespoons Butter or 1 tablespoon oil + 1 or 2 tablespoons butter
  2. 1 tej patta (Indian bay leaf), optional
  3. ▢ ½ to 1 teaspoon kashmiri red chili powder or deghi mirch or ¼ to ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper or paprika
  4. 1.5 cups water or add as required
  5. ▢ 1 inch ginger – peeled and julienned, reserve a few for garnish
  6. ▢ 1 or 2 green chili – slit, reserve a few for garnish
  7. ▢ 200 to 250 grams Paneer (Indian cottage cheese) – cubed or diced
  8. ▢ 1 teaspoon dry fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi) – optional
  9. ▢ ½ to 1 teaspoon Garam Masala or tandoori masala
  10. ▢ 2 to 3 tablespoons light cream or half & half or 1 to 2 tablespoons heavy cream – optional
  11. ▢ ¼ to 1 teaspoon sugar – optional, add as required depending on the sourness of the tomatoes
  12. ▢ salt as required

For garnish

  1. ▢ 1 to 2 tablespoons coriander leaves – chopped, (cilantro) – optional
  2. ▢ 1 inch ginger – peeled and julienned
  3. ▢ 1 tablespoon light cream or 1 tablespoon heavy cream – optional
  4. ▢ 1 to 2 teaspoons Butter – optional
